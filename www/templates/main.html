Best of EAST and WEST

Passion Driven Learning

At Hwa Chong, students enjoy a holistic education that puts them in touch with the dynamics and challenges of the real world. Every Hwa Chong student receives a rigorous curriculum that emphasises leadership, character building, academic excellence and creativity.

Character and Leadership
